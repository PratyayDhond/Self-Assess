Frequently Asked Questions

What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered corona virus.

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. Some patients may have aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhea. These symptoms are usually mild and begin gradually.

Is it safe to follow the diagnosis of the self-assessment test?

The self-assessment test is just a guideline and not an actual diagnosis and should not be taken as a medical advice. If you feel any pain, discomfort or any symptom it is recommended that you visit a doctor immediately.
